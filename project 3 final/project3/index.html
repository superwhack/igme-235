javasqueebs!

willkommen!

welcome to javasqueebs, a short idle game about collecting creatures called squeebs. this page will break down the mechanics, and you can always return to it by pressing the [?] button in the bottom left

collection

this is where you can view your squeebs! beneath each squeeb's picture is their name and modifier, which changes how fast they complete activities. try to collect all 24! you start with the one and only prime squeeb. bask in its might and glory!

materials

this is where you can see your materials that you've collected. squeebs gather materials when adventuring, and you can spend materials to find new places to adventure.

adventure

venture out into a vast squorld, discovering different squeebs and gathering materials. click on the drop-down to select a squeeb, and click start to begin adventuring!

grading & documentation

because this game has time based mechanics, you can click a button in the bottom right of this screen to make all adventures go extra fast. you may have to restart the adventure to make this work. due to the nature of random chance, you still might not get everything, but everything is possible to get. documentation. mock.
